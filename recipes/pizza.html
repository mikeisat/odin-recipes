How to make the best Pizza

Pizza is easier to make then you think and it's healither than buying store pizza.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Mozzerella
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Make pizza dough with flour
  2. Slather tomato sauce on dough
  3. Sprinkle mozzerella on top of the sauce
  4. Put in oven on 400F for 30 minutes
