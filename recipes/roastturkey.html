Roast Turkey

How to Prepare a Roast Turkey

The holidays are around the corner and the Roast Turkey is a cherished tradition during this time of year. The following recipe will teach you how to cook the perfect Roast Turkey.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Turkey
  • Kosher Salt
  • Coarse Pepper
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Brine Turkey
  2. Rub Salt and Pepper over turkey skin
  3. Pre-heat oven to 400F
  4. Place Turkey in oven for 4 hours
  5. Take out turkey and let it sit for 2 hours
